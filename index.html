Privacy Policy

Patient Monitor (com.alaanasef.patientmonitor)
Developer: Alaa Alnasef · Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Overview

Patient Monitor is an application intended for use by authorized healthcare professionals to record and review patient information (demographics, medical history, vital signs, laboratory values, medications, clinical notes, and related images) within their care team. The app is distributed through closed/managed channels and is not intended for the general public or for patients themselves.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information (app users / clinicians): email address, display name, and role, used to authenticate staff accounts and control access.
  • Patient health information (entered by clinicians): demographics, medical history, vital signs, laboratory results, medications, clinical notes, and clinical images. This information is entered by authorized staff for care-coordination purposes.
  • Device push token: a Firebase Cloud Messaging registration token used to deliver notifications.

The app does not collect location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, or browsing data, and it contains no advertising.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate authorized users and enforce role-based access.
  • To store and synchronize patient records across the care team in real time.
  • To deliver notifications relevant to patient care.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing or advertising.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Data is stored in Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage) operated by Google LLC, acting as a data processor. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest on Google's infrastructure. Access is restricted to authenticated, authorized accounts through Firebase security rules.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Records are retained while the account or patient record remains active in the system. Administrators can remove user accounts and patient records from within the app. You may also request deletion of your account or of data concerning you by contacting us at the address below; we will action verified requests within 30 days.

6. Responsibility of the Healthcare Organization

The healthcare team or organization using Patient Monitor is responsible for ensuring that its use of the app — including obtaining any required patient consent and complying with applicable healthcare privacy laws in its jurisdiction — is lawful.

7. Children

The app is intended for use by adult healthcare professionals only and is not directed at children.

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
